# Admin Dashboard Manual

This manual explains every Admin Dashboard tab in detail. For each tab, you will find:
1. Purpose
2. What you can see
3. What you can do
4. Common actions

---

## Dashboard
Purpose: Quick health overview for the platform.

What you can see:
1. KPI cards (users, reviews, ratings, points, etc. depending on enabled widgets).
2. Recent reviews and recent branches.
3. Charts for activity trends (reviews/ratings).

What you can do:
1. Monitor overall system activity at a glance.
2. Export dashboard reports (CSV/PDF) when available.

Common actions:
1. Check spikes in reviews or rating changes.
2. Open a review from the recent list to moderate it.

---

## Categories
Purpose: Manage the master list of categories and subcategories.

What you can see:
1. Categories list with status (active/inactive).
2. Subcategories for each category.

What you can do:
1. Create, edit, activate/deactivate, or delete categories.
2. Create, edit, activate/deactivate, or delete subcategories.

Common actions:
1. Add a new category for a new business sector.
2. Disable a category to hide it from the app without deleting it.

---

## Brands
Purpose: Manage brands and their profiles.

What you can see:
1. Brands list with status.
2. Brand details including logo, cover, and subcategory.

What you can do:
1. Create and update brand info.
2. Upload brand logo and cover image.
3. Assign subcategory to a brand.
4. Create or update a Brand Admin account.
5. Activate/deactivate or delete brands.

Common actions:
1. Add a brand and assign its admin user.
2. Disable a brand temporarily.

---

## Places
Purpose: Manage places that can be reviewed.

What you can see:
1. Places list with brand and subcategory info.
2. Active/inactive status.

What you can do:
1. Create and edit places.
2. Upload place logo and cover image.
3. Activate/deactivate or delete places.

Common actions:
1. Create a standalone place (not attached to a brand).
2. Update place photos and descriptions.

---

## Branches
Purpose: Manage branch locations and QR codes.

What you can see:
1. Branch list with place and brand info.
2. Branch address and review cooldown days.
3. QR code generation status.

What you can do:
1. Create and edit branches.
2. Set review cooldown days per branch.
3. Generate and view QR codes.
4. Download QR codes as PDF.
5. Activate/deactivate or delete branches.

Common actions:
1. Generate a QR code after adding a new branch.
2. Update review cooldown to control rating frequency.

---

## Reviews
Purpose: Moderate and manage user reviews.

What you can see:
1. Review list with filters.
2. Review details with answers and photos.
3. Status indicators (hidden, featured).

What you can do:
1. Hide/unhide reviews.
2. Feature/unfeature reviews.
3. Reply to reviews.
4. Export reviews (CSV/PDF).
5. Open review details.

Common actions:
1. Hide inappropriate content.
2. Feature high-quality reviews.

---

## Users
Purpose: View user activity and profiles.

What you can see:
1. User list with status.
2. User profile details, points, and reviews.

What you can do:
1. Open user details and review history.

Common actions:
1. Investigate reports of abuse or suspicious activity.

---

## Lookups
Purpose: Manage reference data used across the app.

What you can see:
1. Genders, nationalities, cities, areas.

What you can do:
1. Add, edit, activate/deactivate, or delete lookup values.

Common actions:
1. Add new city/area when expanding coverage.

---

## Rewards System
Purpose: Manage points rules and user levels.

What you can see:
1. Active points rule version.
2. Historical rules list.
3. User levels and benefits.

What you can do:
1. Create a new rules version.
2. Activate a rules version.
3. Add, edit, or delete user levels.

Common actions:
1. Increase points per review in a new rules version.
2. Add a new user level for high activity users.

---

## Rating Questions
Purpose: Manage rating criteria and question weights.

What you can see:
1. Questions list by subcategory and type.
2. Question weights and points.

What you can do:
1. Add/edit rating questions.
2. Configure weights.
3. Configure scoring for rating/yes-no/multiple choice.

Common actions:
1. Adjust weights to prioritize key criteria.
2. Add new question for a specific subcategory.

---

## Subscription Plans
Purpose: Manage subscription packages.

What you can see:
1. Plans list with price, trial days, status, and best value.

What you can do:
1. Create/edit plans.
2. Activate/deactivate plans.
3. Mark a plan as best value.
4. Delete plan if not used by active subscriptions.

Common actions:
1. Add a new quarterly plan.
2. Update pricing for a plan.

---

## Subscriptions
Purpose: Monitor user subscriptions.

What you can see:
1. Subscriptions list with status and plan.
2. Free until / paid until dates.

What you can do:
1. Filter by status, plan, or date range.

Common actions:
1. Check users whose free period is ending soon.

---

## Banners & Onboarding
Purpose: Manage marketing content for the app.

What you can see:
1. Banners list (image-only).
2. Onboarding screens list (limited to 3).

What you can do:
1. Create/edit/delete banners.
2. Create/edit onboarding screens.

Common actions:
1. Update banner for a new offer.
2. Refresh onboarding text for new campaigns.

---

## Notifications
Purpose: Send push notifications.

What you can see:
1. Notification compose screen.

What you can do:
1. Send to a single user.
2. Send to multiple users.

Common actions:
1. Announce new brand or campaign.

---

## Subscription Settings
Purpose: Control free trial duration.

What you can see:
1. Free trial days value.

What you can do:
1. Update free trial days for new users.

Common actions:
1. Set trial to 90 days for a promotion.

---

## Vouchers
Purpose: Track voucher usage and status.

What you can see:
1. Voucher list with status.
2. Filters and summary stats.

What you can do:
1. Export CSV.
2. Filter by brand/status/date.

Common actions:
1. Check voucher usage during campaigns.

---

## Featured Places
Purpose: Select featured places shown in the app.

What you can see:
1. Places list with featured status.

What you can do:
1. Mark/unmark places as featured.

Common actions:
1. Promote specific places in campaigns.

---

## Search Suggestions
Purpose: Manage manual search suggestions.

What you can see:
1. Suggestions list with status and order.

What you can do:
1. Add/edit/delete suggestions.
2. Activate/deactivate suggestions.
3. Change sort order.

Common actions:
1. Add seasonal keywords for promotions.

---

## KPI Reports
Purpose: View consolidated KPIs.

What you can see:
1. Subscription KPIs.
2. Points KPIs.
3. QR scan KPIs.
4. Top and low rated branches.

What you can do:
1. Filter by date range.

Common actions:
1. Review performance before monthly reports.

---

## QR Management
Purpose: Central management of branch QR codes.

What you can see:
1. All branches with QR codes.
2. Filters by brand and status.

What you can do:
1. Export CSV for QR codes.
2. View QR image.
3. Download QR PDF.

Common actions:
1. Bulk export QR codes for printing.

## Admins (Super Admin)
Purpose: Manage admin accounts.

What you can see:
1. Admin list with status.

What you can do:
1. Create/edit admins.
2. Activate/deactivate admins.
3. Delete admins (if permitted).

Common actions:
1. Add a new admin for content moderation.
